The Story Behind the Stitches

Karefully Crafted began in 2018 as a passion project in my Rexburg home. What started as a way to create meaningful gifts for friends and family quickly grew into something more when I discovered how crochet could be a vehicle for positive change.

After learning about the incredible work of the Upper Valley Child Advocacy Center, I knew I wanted to use my craft to support their mission. Each piece I create is infused with intention - not just to bring joy to the recipient, but to provide resources for children who need it most.

Every item is made with natural, sustainable materials and designed to last for generations. From selecting the perfect yarn to adding the final stitch, I pour my heart into each creation knowing it serves a greater purpose.

About the Upper Valley Child Advocacy Center

Located in Rigby, ID, the Upper Valley Child Advocacy Center provides a safe, child-friendly environment for children who have experienced abuse.

Their multidisciplinary team works to reduce trauma, promote healing, and seek justice through coordinated investigations, forensic interviews, medical evaluations, and therapeutic services.

Your support helps provide:

  • Comfort items like blankets and stuffed animals
  • Art and play therapy supplies
  • Forensic interview equipment
  • Staff training and development
  • Community education programs
